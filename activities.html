    USC Fisher Museum of Art

    Yoga while surrounded by art, the painting and sculpture collection SEA SKY LAND: towards a map of everything, a poetic sculpture/engraving with a specially crafted garden design, USC’s Fisher Museum of Art provides beautiful pieces to relax and inspire the mind. Give your eyes and body a break and refresh with wonderful, thought-provoking, gorgeous art!

    Location: 823 South Exposition Boulevard Los Angeles CA, 90089
    Hours: Tuesday - Friday 12 PM - 5 PM, Saturday 12 PM - 4 PM Reservation Needed

    Location: 3014 West Olympic Boulevard
    Hours: Sunrise to Sunset
