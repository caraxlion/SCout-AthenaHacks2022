    K-Town Day Trip

    MaDang Courtyard

    Location: 621 S Western Ave Los Angeles, CA 90005

    Transportation: Pennypinchers, you can take the bus for free

    Opening Hours: 8 AM - 11 PM

    Into kpop? Looking for the new Twice album? IU album? Head to Kpop Music Town, which offers a beautiful variety of albums, lightsticks, photocards, stickers and more! Buy that album you've been eyeing, and you'll get a poster for $3! Nearby, you'll find a Daiso, home of the cheap yet cute, a Somi-Somi for your taiyaki addiction, Paris Baguette for breakfast, pastries and cake, a chicken restaurant, shaved ice, sushi offerings and more! MaDang Courtyard has it all for a wonderful afternoon, satisfying and delighting all your senses!

    Picnic Day

    Exposition Park

    Location: 700 Exposition Park Drive Los Angeles, CA 90037

    Transportation: Walk, it's close enough

    Opening Hours: 6 AM - 10 PM

    Featuring roses in bloom all-year long, Exposition Park is gorgeous and a short walk campus, perfect for anyone who doesn’t feel like going far. Grab your trusty blanket and prepare some snacks and sandwiches for a perfect lazy, sunny afternoon amidst beautiful flowers. If you want to do a little more, walk a bit farther to find the breadth of Exposition Museums, from the Natural History Museum to the California African American Museum. Check out activities for more to do there. Either way, prepare for a delightful day either way, with food, company, insight and conversation.

    Peaceful Nature Date

    Griffith Park Observatory

    Location: 2800 East Observatory Road Los Angeles, CA 90027

    Transportation: Bus is free, Uber if you can and want to pay

    Opening Hours: Weekends - 10 AM - 10 PM Weekdays - 12 PM - 10 PM

    Need a simple, relaxing getaway from the stress of school? Midterms finally done and ready to treat your exhausted self? Check out our picnic food recommendations or hit up your nearby Ralphs or H-Mart and prepare a feast for Griffith Park Observatory where you can enjoy picturesque views and destress with the serenity of nature. Explore further around the massive park, with hiking trails and magnificent trees galore and admire the austere light of the stars from the observatory.

    Shopping Trip

    Westfield Century City Mall

    Location: 10250 Santa Monica Blvd, Los Angeles, CA 90067

    Transportation: Bus or Uber (~$20)

    Opening Hours: 10 AM - 8 PM

    This magnificent 3-story shopping complex boasts live music, food, and shopping for the parsimonious and the splurging. Come admire the natural scenery and revamp your closet! Wander around, window-shop a bit, and dig out some excellent hidden gems and your next statement piece!
